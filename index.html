What We Do

RoboMaster at Berkeley is a club that provides a robotics sports community to Cal students. Different from other robotic clubs focusing on developing the most advanced strategy, we aim to provide an opportunity and resources for students from all backgrounds to fulfill their creative ideas in all terms of algorithm, hardware, AI, signal, control, service, social impact, etc.

Meet Our Team BEAR DYNAMICS

The story of our team is boosting the creativity, impact and uniqueness of Robomaster @ Berkeley. Our Journey begins here: The thing we are doing now may make us the coolest robotics team in Berkeley.

The Mission We Bear: For all club members and students who want to start with robotics, we will provide equipments, tutorials and competition opportunities. We wish expensive and advanced robotic technology/devices to be accessible for all students interested. Inclusion and Competition: Our club uses ICRA Robomaster AI Challenge as the guidance through the year. AS we bear the mission to provide access to robotics to all Berkeley students interested, we release tutorials for robotic techniques in fields of computer vision/machine learning/ROS control through YouTube Channel and Facebook Post to all students. We are creating a playground for engineer students, and thus, inclusion and fun.
